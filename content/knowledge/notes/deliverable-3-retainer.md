## Retainer Model Structure

### Output-Oriented Framework

> "A bundled fee is negotiated for services like technical and creative direction, with deliverables and priorities negotiated at touch points with the client." — Kev Freeney

### Key Principles

1. **Not time-based billing:** Value delivered through expertise, not hours logged
2. **Prioritization at touch points:** Weekly/bi-weekly alignment on priorities
3. **Producer-mediated:** Jochem van Bruggen coordinates resource allocation
4. **Commissioning model:** Specific deliverables can be commissioned with time estimates

## IR35 Compliance Considerations

### Structure for IR35 Safety

| Factor | IDIRNET Retainer Design |
|--------|-------------------------|
| **Substitution** | Explicit right to substitute with qualified personnel (Kris, Jochem, Colm, network collaborators) |
| **Control** | Deliverables-focused; Lightheart controls WHAT, IDIRNET controls HOW |
| **Financial Risk** | IDIRNET invoices and bears risk of defective work |
| **Exclusivity** | Non-exclusive; IDIRNET free to serve other clients |
| **Equipment** | IDIRNET provides specialist equipment where possible |

### Assessment

**Option (c) Partnership Agreement with IDIRNET presents LOWER IR35 RISK as a genuine B2B service arrangement.**
